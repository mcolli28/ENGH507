Places I have lived

I was born in Arkansas, but moved to Virginia just before starting kindergarten. To this day I am still the only person I have met from Arkansas.

I grew up in southwest Virginia. I know the cities and towns of this part of Virginina well; however, when I tell people from northen Virginia this is where I grew up, I generally just tell them I grew up near Virginia Tech (which was about a 30-minute rive south from where i lved.)

For college, I moved to Newport News, VA ( for more information on this time in my life, check out the education History page)

I moved to northern Virginia after college to begin work. Although I got my first job directly out of college working for a local newspaper, I quickly joined many of my peers and friends who either moved to northern Virginina after college or were originally from here and moved back.

After spenmding a few years in northern Virginia, I wanted a change of pace, so I moved to San Diego without much of a plan. I lived by the beach tehre from early 2017 to mid 2018, a total of 18 months. I had a great time there and miss the beach every day.
