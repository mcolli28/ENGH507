Personal Biography

On this page, I'd like to share some of my experiences with you. To do so, I have organized my page into three sections, seen below.

Favorite Foods

Food plays a very important role in my life. Not only does it keep me alive, but I typically enjoy eating it. Some of my favorite foods include:

  • Pizza
  • Burgers
  • Chipotle, which is not a food type, but is a chain so important to me it supersedes the product it offers

Places I have lived

I was born in Arkansas, but moved to Virginia just before starting kindergarten. To this day I am still the only person I have met from Arkansas.

I grew up in southwest Virginia. I know the cities and towns of this part of Virginia well; however, when I tell people from northern Virginia this is where I grew up, I generally just tell them I grew up near Virginia Tech (which was about a 30-minute drive south from where I lived.)

For college, I moved to Newport News, VA (for more information on this time in my life, check out the education History page)

I moved to northern Virginia after college to begin work. Although I got my first job directly out of college working for a local newspaper, I quickly joined many of my peers and friends who either moved to northern Virginia after college or were originally from here and moved back.

After spending a few years in northern Virginia, I wanted a change of pace, so I moved to San Diego without much of a plan. I lived by the beach there from early 2017 to mid 2018, a total of 18 months. I had a great time there and miss the beach every day.

San Diego Fact

While in San Diego, I took up surfing. It was a really fun activity, but just as I was getting the hang of it I was stung by a stingray and became paranoid about walking on the ocean floor. Ironically, if I had been stricter about my surfing, I would have paddled out instead of walking out, avoiding the injury.

My Interests

I'm interested in many things, but given the current state of the world, I've become increasingly interested in activities that can be done in isolation and how I can avoid public transportation. One of my more recent hobbies has been cycling. I've had a bicycle for a few years that has been gathering dust, but it has seen more use recently than ever before. The trails around the national capital region are quite impressive and you really have quite a bit of access to many places.
