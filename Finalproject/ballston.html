Description of Republik

Nestled in an interior courtyard, out of view from both the street and the metro, this local coffee spot is worth a little searching to find. For commuters that live in Ballston on the west side of N Glebe Road and use the metro to get to work, this is an ideal spot to stop before getting on the train. If you work in Ballston, Republik can serve as a great pick-me-up after a delicious yet sleep-inducing lunch from its neighbor Big Buns. Formerly known as Rock ‘n’ Joe Coffee Bar when it opened in 2016, the owners rebranded the shop as Republik Coffe Bar in 2017. The interior offers a cool and quiet spot to take a moment before getting on with your day, no matter when you seek out your daily dose of caffeine.
