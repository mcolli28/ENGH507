Description of Coffee Beanery

Don’t be tempted by the convenience of Starbucks right outside the Virginia Square Metro station. The Coffee Beanery is a family-owned coffee shop is located just a block away from the Virginia Square Metro station. Coffee Beanery offers a variety of prepared coffee, teas, and specialty drinks. Not to mention, they produce and distribute their own beans, so you know they’ll be experts in preparing your drinks and making recommendations. The shop also sells their beans on site, so you can purchase their beans from this location. You can enjoy your Friday morning commute cup on Saturday and Sunday too!
