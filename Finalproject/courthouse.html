Description of Brooklyn Bagel Bakery

Although specialists in authentic New York bagels, Brooklyn Bagel Bakery offers a variety of simpler coffee options that are sure to pair perfectly with a bagel. Their boxed java to go comes with enough coffee to fuel a small office in the morning, and if you are really trying to score some brownie points with your coworkers, you’d be wise to stock up on a dozen bagels as well! Depending on which exit you use at the Courthouse Metro stop, you’ll only be a few short steps away too!
