Description of Northside Social

The Clarendon Metro stop might not be the first place people think about working and early morning commutes, but there are a large amount of residential and office properties scattered above the usually weekend businesses. Luckily, Northside Social, just a few blocks west of the Clarendon Metro stop is a local favorite and a veritable restaurant on top of being a beloved coffee shop. Offering brunch, dinner, wine, and coffee, Northside Social is worth the stop morning, afternoon, or night any day of the week.
